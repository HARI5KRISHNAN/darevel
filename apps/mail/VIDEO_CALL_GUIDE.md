# How to Video Call with Alice (Multi-User Guide)

## Quick Start - Video Call with Alice

### Method 1: Share Meeting Link (Easiest)

1. **You start the meeting:**
   - Click **"Schedule Meeting"** in sidebar
   - Click **"Start & Join Now"** button
   - Wait for video call to open (max 10 seconds)
   - Click **"Copy Link"** button in the top header

2. **Alice joins the meeting:**
   - You send the copied link to Alice via email/chat
   - Alice opens the link in her browser: `https://meet.jit.si/XXXXXX`
   - Alice allows camera/microphone when prompted
   - ‚úÖ Alice joins your video call!

---

### Method 2: Same Room Name

1. **You start the meeting:**
   - Note down the **Room Name** shown at top of video call
   - Example: `Room: abc123def456`

2. **Alice joins:**
   - Alice goes to: `https://meet.jit.si/abc123def456`
   - Alice enters her name and joins
   - ‚úÖ You both are in the same video call!

---

### Method 3: Schedule Meeting for Later

1. **Schedule meeting:**
   - Go to **"Schedule Meeting"**
   - Fill in details (title, time, attendees)
   - Add Alice's email: `alice@pilot180.local`
   - Click **"Generate Meeting Link"**
   - Click **"Schedule Meeting"**

2. **Both join from Calendar:**
   - Go to **"Calendar"** tab
   - Click on the scheduled meeting
   - Click **"Join Meeting"** button
   - ‚úÖ Both of you join the same room!

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Video call stuck on loading

**Fixes:**
1. **Check internet connection** - Jitsi needs internet to work
2. **Allow camera/microphone** - Must grant permissions
3. **Wait 10 seconds** - Loading screen auto-hides after 10s
4. **Check browser console** - Press F12 ‚Üí Console tab for errors
5. **Try different browser** - Use Chrome or Edge (best support)

### Issue: Alice can't join

**Fixes:**
1. **Check link format** - Should be: `https://meet.jit.si/ROOM_NAME`
2. **Verify room name** - Must match exactly (case-sensitive)
3. **Check Alice's internet** - She needs internet connection
4. **Try different browser** - Chrome/Edge recommended

### Issue: Can't see each other

**Fixes:**
1. **Check camera permissions** - Both must allow camera access
2. **Click camera icon** - Might be muted by default
3. **Check browser settings** - Allow camera in browser settings

### Issue: Can't hear each other

**Fixes:**
1. **Check microphone permissions** - Both must allow mic access
2. **Click mic icon** - Might be muted
3. **Check audio device** - Select correct mic in settings

## Best Practices

1. **Use headphones** - Reduces echo and feedback
2. **Good lighting** - Face a window or light source
3. **Stable internet** - WiFi or ethernet connection
4. **Close other tabs** - Frees up CPU/RAM
5. **Chrome/Edge browser** - Best Jitsi performance
